# United Kingdom Payment Regulations

## Overview
The UK has a highly developed payments ecosystem, heavily influenced by both domestic innovation (like the New Payments Architecture) and retained EU legislation post-Brexit. The regulatory framework emphasizes consumer protection, competition, and open banking.

## Primary Regulator(s)
- [Financial Conduct Authority (FCA)](/regulators/fca.md)
- [Bank of England (BoE)](/regulators/boe.md)

## Key Payment Systems
- Faster Payments Service (FPS)
- Bacs
- CHAPS
- New Payments Architecture (NPA)

## Major Regulations
- [Payment Services Regulations 2017](/countries/united-kingdom/payment-services-regulations-2017.md)

## Recent Developments
- Ongoing transition to the New Payments Architecture (NPA) and ISO 20022.
- Enhanced Confirmation of Payee (CoP) mandates for fraud prevention.
