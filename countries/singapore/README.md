# Singapore Payment Regulations

## Overview
Singapore is a leading global financial hub with a highly advanced and forward-looking payment ecosystem. The regulatory framework is designed to foster innovation while ensuring strict consumer protection and financial stability.

## Primary Regulator(s)
- [Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS)](/regulators/mas.md)

## Key Payment Systems
- FAST (Fast And Secure Transfers)
- PayNow
- MEPS+ (MAS Electronic Payment System)

## Major Regulations
- [Payment Services Act](/countries/singapore/payment-services-act.md)

## Recent Developments
- Continued push for cross-border QR code payments in ASEAN.
- Enhanced AML/CFT guidelines for digital payment token (DPT) service providers.
